Food is one of the basic necessities of life. Food contains nutrients—substances essential for the growth, repair, and maintenance of body tissues and for the regulation of vital processes.

Burger

A hamburger or burger is an American fast food. It's a type of sandwich with a patty of cooked ground meat between the two halves of a bun.


  

Dosa

A dosa is a thin, savoury crepe in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice.

  

Biryani

Biryani is prepared from basmati rice mixed with several spices and cooked in a special way.
